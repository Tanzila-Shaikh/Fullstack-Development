About Mental Health Predictor

Mental Health Predictor is a revolutionary application dedicated to empowering individuals to understand their mental well-being. Our goal is to provide a comprehensive platform that offers insights into various mental disorders, helping users take proactive steps towards mental wellness.

Key Features

  • Predicts four major types of mental disorders.
  • Provides personalized insights based on user input.
  • Offers resources and recommendations for mental well-being.
  • Secure and confidential platform to ensure user privacy.

Our Team

Our team consists of dedicated professionals passionate about mental health and technology. We combine expertise in psychology, data science, and software development to create a user-friendly and effective platform for mental wellness.
